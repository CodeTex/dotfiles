---
name: adr-writing
description: Write short, practical architecture decision records with explicit trade-offs
---

## ADR shape

```markdown
# ADR-NNN: Short Title

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Status**: proposed | accepted | deprecated | superseded

## Context
What problem or pressure exists?

## Decision
What was chosen?

## Consequences
- Good:
- Bad:
- Neutral:
```

## Rules

- Keep it short.
- Capture one decision per ADR.
- Name trade-offs directly.
- Prefer practical consequences over ceremony.
